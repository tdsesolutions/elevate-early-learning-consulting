Meet the Heart Behind Elevate Early Learning

Our Story

Elevate Early Learning & Consulting was founded by Ms. Tanisha Butler, a passionate early childhood professional with a Master's Degree in Education and over a decade of hands-on experience in the child care industry.

Licensed by the Texas Department of Family and Protective Services, Tanisha has dedicated her career to ensuring that child care centers across Texas operate with the highest standards of safety, quality, and educational excellence.

Her mission is simple yet powerful: to ensure safety, quality, and assurance—one child, one center, and one director at a time.

Comprehensive Solutions for Your Child Care Center

Our Services

From licensing preparation to curriculum development, we provide expert guidance tailored to your center's unique needs.

Policies & Procedures

Tailored development and implementation designed to strengthen operations, improve structure, and ensure compliance.

Risk Management

Comprehensive strategies to identify potential risks and establish preventative systems that protect children, staff, and your center.

Opening Inspections

Expert preparation and guidance to ensure your center meets regulatory standards with confidence and success.

CACFP Guidance

In-depth support for nutrition standards, compliance, and program management under the Child and Adult Care Food Program.

Menu Planning

Balanced, nutritious meal planning designed to meet guidelines while supporting the needs of children.

Curriculum Development

Engaging, age-appropriate educational programs that promote learning, structure, and development.

Classroom Management

Practical strategies to maintain a positive, structured, and productive learning environment.

Texas Rising Star & Texas School Ready Support

Assistance navigating program requirements, expectations, and qualification processes.
